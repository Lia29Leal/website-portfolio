SDG #13 is essential for addressing the global crisis of climate change, focusing on reducing emissions and fostering climate resilience. Students can contribute by applying their skills in science and technology to develop renewable energy solutions or sustainable infrastructure. Projects like energy-efficient designs or climate research align with this goal, ensuring that future generations help build a more sustainable world.
